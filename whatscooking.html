Dinner Recipes for the Week

Monday — Hidden‑Veggie Beef Pasta

Ingredients
  • Whole‑wheat pasta
  • Lean beef mince
  • Passata
  • Carrot, courgette, paprika (grated or finely diced)
  • Onion, garlic
  • Olive oil, oregano, basil
Instructions
  • Cook pasta according to package.
  • Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil.
  • Add beef mince and brown it.
  • Add grated veggies and cook until soft.
  • Pour in passata, season with oregano and basil.
  • Simmer 10 minutes and mix with pasta.

Tuesday — Soft Chicken Tacos

Ingredients
  • Chicken breast or thighs
  • Whole‑wheat tortillas
  • Lettuce, tomato, avocado
  • Black beans or corn
  • Yoghurt + lime for sauce
  • Spices: paprika, cumin, garlic powder
Instructions
  • Slice chicken and season with paprika, cumin, garlic powder.
  • Pan‑fry until cooked through.
  • Warm tortillas in a dry pan.
  • Prep toppings: chop lettuce, tomato, avocado.
  • Mix yoghurt + lime for a simple sauce.
  • Let kids assemble their own tacos.

Wednesday — Oven‑Baked White Fish with Potatoes & Green Beans

Ingredients
  • White fish fillets (kabeljauw, koolvis, pangasius)
  • Potatoes (wedges or cubes)
  • Green beans
  • Lemon, olive oil, parsley
Instructions
  • Preheat oven to 200°C.
  • Toss potatoes with olive oil, salt, pepper; bake 25–30 min.
  • Steam or boil green beans.
  • Place fish on a tray, drizzle with olive oil and lemon.
  • Bake fish for 12–15 minutes.
  • Serve with parsley and lemon.

Thursday — Chicken & Veggie Fried Rice

Ingredients
  • Cooked brown rice
  • Chicken breast or thigh
  • Eggs
  • Peas, carrots, corn, spring onion
  • Soy sauce (low salt), tiny splash sesame oil
Instructions
  • Cook rice earlier or use leftovers.
  • Dice chicken and cook in a pan.
  • Add veggies and stir‑fry.
  • Push aside, scramble eggs in the same pan.
  • Add rice, soy sauce, and mix everything.
  • Finish with spring onions.

Friday — Mini Pita Pizzas

Ingredients
  • Whole‑wheat mini pitas
  • Passata or tomato puree
  • Mozzarella
  • Toppings: paprika, sweetcorn, spinach, chicken pieces
  • Oregano
Instructions
  • Preheat oven to 200°C.
  • Spread passata on each pita.
  • Add cheese and toppings.
  • Sprinkle oregano.
  • Bake 8–10 minutes.
  • Let kids design their own pizzas.
